Chris Klosowski is a Software Developer specializing in PHP, WordPress, eCommerce, and social media. His primary role is as the Co-Lead developer of Easy Digital Downloads, the easiest way to sell digital products with WordPress.

When not writing code, you can find him blogging about Software Development and hosting the RFC Podcast, a bi-weekly show geared towards managing a career in software development.

Chris currently lives in Queen Creek, Arizona with his wife and two sons. In his free time he enjoys brewing beer and teaching his sons the ways of the force. Chris holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Electronic Imaging from Northen Michigan University.
