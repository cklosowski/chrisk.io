After years on WordPress, I moved this site to something lighter. No database,
no plugin updates, no admin to keep patched — just my writing, turned into plain
HTML and served from the edge.

I write here about three things:

- **Findings** — things I learn that are worth writing down.
- **Experiments** — small bets, prototypes, and what came of them.
- **Leadership** — how teams and products actually get built.

If you're curious how the site itself works: posts are Markdown files in a Git
repository. I edit them in a browser, hit publish, and a static site is rebuilt
and deployed automatically a few seconds later. Boring, fast, and durable —
which is exactly what I wanted.
