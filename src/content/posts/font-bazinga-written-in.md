Yep, that's a search term that ended up at this website (most likely due to [this post](https://chrisk.io/matching-bazinga-shirts/ "Matching Bazinga Shirts")) and I hate to disappoint visitors. According to WhatTheFont.com, the expression spouted by one Doctor Sheldon Cooper after someone falls victim to one of his 'ruses' is written in **Bada Boom Pro BB Italic**. I'm not crazy, my mother had me tested...
