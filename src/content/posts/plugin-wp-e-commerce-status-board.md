A while back I wrote about [integrating WordPress plugins with Panic's Status Board iPad App](https://chrisk.io/2013/05/integrating-status-board-with-wordpress-plugins/), and while the plugin has been soft launched for about a month while I listen for user feedback...I'm happy to announce that [WP e-Commerce Status Board is available on the WordPress.org repo](http://wordpress.org/plugins/wpec-status-board/). With this Status Board widget, you'll be able to track your sales and earnings in WP e-Commerce on one graph, on your iPad:

[![screenshot-1](/images/blog/screenshot-1-234x300-1.png)](/images/blog/screenshot-1-1.png)

To add this widget to Status Board, simply login to your WP e-Commerce site on your iPad, view your Sales Logs page, and at the bottom of the page is a button to add your stats to Status Board.

If you have any questions or issues check out the contextual help at the top right of the Sales Logs page and if that doesn't help, please use the [WordPress.org Support Forum](http://wordpress.org/support/plugin/wpec-status-board).

A big thanks to [Dan Milward](https://twitter.com/danmilward) and [Justin Sainton](https://twitter.com/JS_Zao) for their discussions and testing.
